# **App Name**: SparkLux Academics

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Homepage Visuals: An aesthetic homepage featuring an automatic left-to-right sliding image carousel, showcasing department facilities, student projects, and key achievements to immediately engage visitors.
- Essential Information Sections: Dedicated and professionally designed pages for 'About Us' providing departmental overview, 'Contact Us' with necessary contact information, and a dynamic 'Department News' section for announcements and updates.
- Visual Achievement Gallery: A sophisticated gallery section to display high-quality photographs of departmental achievements, campus events, and student activities, offering a visual testament to excellence.
- Secure User Authentication: Robust signup and sign-in functionalities for both students and teachers, enabling secure access to personalized content and ensuring data privacy for registered users.
- Personalized Academic Record Viewer: A secure dashboard allowing authenticated students to view their individual academic performance records, including internal examination marks, semester results, and attendance, categorized by examination type (e.g., '1st Internal', 'Semester') and semester number.
- AI-Powered Electrical Engineering Tool: An intelligent tool providing comprehensive explanations, definitions, and relevant academic resources for various electrical engineering topics, designed to assist students with complex concepts and study.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Dark theme, exuding luxury, professionalism, and high-tech elegance.
- Primary accent color: A rich, deep indigo blue (#2E2EB2), representing technological depth and sophistication, contrasting against the dark background.
- Background color: A very dark, desaturated navy (#1F1F2E), providing a premium canvas that allows other elements to stand out.
- Secondary accent color: A vibrant electric cyan (#61DBFF), used sparingly for highlights, interactive elements, and to symbolize electrical energy and innovation, analogous to the primary color.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a modern, techy, and precise feel, suitable for department titles and feature headers.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), for its clean, neutral, and highly readable design, ensuring clarity for academic content and general information.
- Sleek, minimalist line icons that align with a high-tech and modern aesthetic, ensuring visual clarity without distracting from content.
- Clean, structured, and responsive layout with generous negative space to enhance the luxurious feel and improve readability across all devices. Emphasize a strong hero section for the dynamic carousel.
- Subtle and fluid micro-interactions, such as smooth transitions for the image carousel, hover effects on navigation items, and content fades, adding to the sophisticated and polished user experience without being obtrusive.
